Who We Are

ELSOL.ie an e-learning consultancy and development company based in Dublin, serving clients nationwide.

We provide e-learning project management, development and support services across industry sectors for our clients. Uniquely, we focus on making learning and performance technologies work for you in your business as you need, using our expertise and guidance.

Whether you require expert input to set you on your way in applying learning technologies to your training strategy or if you want to engage us to manage and implement your solution you will always find us professional and approachable. Our client references are testament to this.

Our Services

We put emphasis on getting to know your business so that the solution we implement will meet your business goals and be measurable. Our understanding of your business is key to our projects successes as it helps us ensure our clients have the new knowledge and skills to make the most of what e-learning can deliver now and into the future.
